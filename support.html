Frequently Asked Questions

To book a ticket:

  1. Go to the Book Ticket page
  2. Select your transport type (train or bus)
  3. Choose your departure and destination locations
  4. Select your travel dates
  5. Enter passenger details
  6. Proceed to payment
  7. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket

Yes, you can modify your booking depending on the ticket type:

  • Flexible tickets: Can be modified up to 24 hours before departure
  • Standard tickets: Can be modified up to 48 hours before departure with a fee
  • Non-refundable tickets: Cannot be modified

To modify a booking, go to My Bookings and select "Modify Booking."

We accept the following payment methods:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Debit cards
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Bank transfers (for corporate accounts)

All transactions are securely processed with 256-bit SSL encryption.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click on "Forgot password?"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 15 minutes, please check your spam folder.
